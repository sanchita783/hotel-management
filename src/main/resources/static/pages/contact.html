FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries

What is the check-in and check-out time? +
Standard check-in is at 2:00 PM and check-out is at 12:00 PM (noon). Early check-in and late check-out are available upon request, subject to availability. Complimentary early check-in from 10 AM for suite guests.
Is breakfast included in the room rate? +
Breakfast is included for Presidential Suite and Executive Suite bookings. For other room types, breakfast can be added for ₹800 per person per day. We serve a full buffet from 7:00 AM to 10:30 AM.
What is your cancellation policy? +
Free cancellation up to 48 hours before check-in date. Cancellations within 48 hours will incur a charge of one night's room rate. No-show bookings will be charged the full booking amount.
Is parking available at the hotel? +
Yes, we have a multi-level secure parking facility. Valet parking is complimentary for Presidential and Executive Suite guests. Self-parking is available for ₹200 per day for other guests.
Do you accept pets? +
We are a pet-friendly hotel. Small pets (under 10 kg) are welcome in designated rooms with a pet fee of ₹500 per night. Please inform us at the time of booking so we can arrange a pet-friendly room for you.
How do I request hotel services during my stay? +
Services can be requested through your guest dashboard after signing in to your account, by calling the front desk (dial 0 from your room phone), or through our mobile app. All requests are tracked and you'll receive real-time updates.
